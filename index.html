About Mahatma Gandhi NREGA Scheme

The Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA) is one of the largest social security initiatives in the world. It guarantees at least 100 days of wage employment in a financial year to every rural household whose adult members volunteer to do unskilled manual work.

Online Services & Digital Updates

With the digitization of the NREGA portal, citizens can now check their job card status, view the active workers list in their Gram Panchayat, verify attendance, and monitor pending payments online.

  • State-Wise Job Card Lists: Access the updated directory of active cards for 2026.
  • Muster Roll Information: Track official work records, attendance sheets, and daily wages.
  • Payment Tracking: Monitor direct benefit transfers (DBT) deposited into post office or bank accounts.

How to Check Your Job Card Status Online

To check your card details, follow these simple steps:

  • Visit the official government web portal or access our detailed guide for step-by-step help.
  • Select your State, Financial Year, District, Block, and Gram Panchayat.
  • Click on the Job Card/Report tab to view the list and search for your household.
